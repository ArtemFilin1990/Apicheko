# Telegram Bot Creative Patterns

## Creative Output Standard

When the user asks for ideas, do not stop at "bot for X". Produce differentiated directions with:

- Bot archetype
- Core promise
- Signature mechanic
- Voice and tone
- Why users return
- Why users share it
- Main risk

Prefer three clearly different directions over three small variations of the same concept.

## Bot Archetypes

- Concierge
  - Fast, premium, minimal friction
  - Good for bookings, lead capture, internal service desks
- Coach
  - Structured progress, encouragement, accountability
  - Good for education, habits, onboarding, productivity
- Curator
  - Filters noise and delivers selected value
  - Good for content bots, research digests, deal discovery
- Operator
  - Transactional, efficient, explicit
  - Good for admin tools, CRM flows, moderation, support routing
- Companion
  - Personality-driven, emotionally sticky, recurring touchpoints
  - Good for communities, wellness, entertainment, fandom
- Game Master
  - Runs loops, quests, rankings, reveals, and progression
  - Good for engagement, referrals, education, community activity

## Signature Mechanics

Use one strong mechanic and build the flow around it.

- Daily drop
  - A new card, task, lead, prompt, or recommendation every day
- Progress path
  - Users unlock stages, chapters, levels, or milestones
- Quest chain
  - Short missions with clear completion criteria
- Streak with purpose
  - Repeated behavior tied to visible benefit, not vanity
- Referral unlock
  - Invite-driven access to content, tools, or higher limits
- Draft-to-human
  - Bot prepares, human confirms or escalates
- Smart inbox
  - Bot collects, scores, and prioritizes items for the user
- Secret menu
  - Power-user commands and deep links for fast access

## Viral And Social Hooks

Use only when it fits the product.

- Shareable result card
  - Summary, score, recommendation, or status the user wants to forward
- Friend challenge
  - Invite a friend to compare, vote, compete, or collaborate
- Referral shortcut
  - Invited users skip onboarding or unlock a better starting point
- Group reveal
  - Content or decision unlocks after a threshold is reached
- Limited drop
  - Time-boxed access that creates urgency without spam

## Tone Directions

Choose one and apply it consistently.

- Sharp operator
  - Short, decisive, utility-first
- Premium concierge
  - Calm, polished, low-noise
- Playful host
  - Warm, clever, slightly theatrical
- Deadpan expert
  - Dry confidence, low emotional amplitude
- Tactical coach
  - Action-heavy, motivating, progress-aware

## Creative Review Questions

Reject the concept if these answers are weak:

- Why would someone remember this bot after one day?
- Why would they reopen it without being forced?
- Why is Telegram specifically a good surface for this product?
- What is the one mechanic users will describe to someone else?
- What would make this feel boring after the second interaction?

## Idea Sprint Format

When the user wants ideation, return:

1. Concept name
2. Archetype
3. Core promise
4. Signature mechanic
5. Sample `/start` copy
6. First three screens
7. Why it can win
8. Why it can fail
